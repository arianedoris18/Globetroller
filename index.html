About Kigali

Kigali is celebrated as one of Africa’s cleanest cities and one of its smallest capitals, spanning just 280 square miles with population of 1.7 million people. Yet within that compact space, the city pulses with diverse energy—from vibrant sports culture to beautiful natural escapes.
